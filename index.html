Self-portrayal

I am currently a student pursuing a bachelors of science in computer science at the University of Denver with minors in mathematics and philosophy. I am particularly interested in the fields of software engineering, natural language processing, and machine learning. For a while after graduating I would like to work in industry developing software, since I love writing code and want to improve at the craft. Eventually I plan to earn a Ph.D. in computer science and continue on to do research and teaching.

I love love love music and some artists I've been listening to a lot recently are Delroy Edwards, (Sandy) Alex G, and Frank Ocean. Check them out if you get the chance.

Most of my coursework so far has been in Java, however I have had decent exposure to C++. My favorite language is definitely Python.

Lately I've been really interested in Aristotle's exploration of eudaimonia and his doctrine of the categories. Also his tomb was possibly discovered recently which is cool.
